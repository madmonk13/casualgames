GEARS
HOW TO PLAY

● Blue gears are sources — always spinning clockwise.

○ Ring targets must spin in the shown direction (↻ or ↺) to light up green.

· Dots are empty pegs. Click to place a gear, click again to remove it.

Adjacent gears always spin opposite ways. You have a limited supply — not every peg needs a gear.

● Red gears mean conflict: two adjacent gears spinning the same way jam each other. Remove one.

Light all targets in the correct direction with no conflicts to solve the level.
