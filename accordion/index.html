🪗 How to Play Accordion

All 52 cards are dealt face-up in a row. Click a card to select it (it glows gold), then click a green-highlighted card to move the pile onto it.

A pile can move 1 or 3 positions to the left if the top cards share the same suit or the same rank.

When a pile moves, everything to its right shifts left — this can open up new 3-left moves that didn't exist before. Think ahead! Moving 1 left is often the wrong call if it blocks a better 3-left move.

The goal is to compress all 52 cards into a single pile. Finishing with fewer than 10 piles is excellent — a perfect game is extremely rare.
