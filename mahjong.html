🀄 How to Play

Goal

Clear all 144 tiles from the board by removing them in matched pairs. Match every tile to win.

Free Tiles

You can only select a tile that is free — meaning nothing is stacked on top of it, and it has at least one open side (left or right). Tiles buried in the stack or fully flanked by neighbors cannot be selected.

Matching

Most tiles match only their identical twin — there are 4 copies of each tile in the deck, so you'll always find at least one pair.

Select one free tile, then select another. If they match, both are removed. If not, the second becomes the new selection.

🌸 Bonus Tiles — Special Matching Rule

There are two bonus groups that play differently. Any tile within a group matches any other tile in that same group — they don't need to be identical.

🌸 Flowers (4 tiles)
🌸 🌺 🌼 🌻
Any flower matches any other flower.
🍂 Seasons (4 tiles)
🌱 🌞 🍂 ❄️
Any season matches any other season.

Stuck?

💡 Hint — flashes a valid matching pair on the board.

↩ Undo — puts the last removed pair back.

🎲 Shuffle — appears when no moves remain; reshuffles remaining tiles in place so you can keep playing.
